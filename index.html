Services

At Tsogolo Lowala, we provide professional sign language interpretation services to ensure effective communication and inclusivity for the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing community. Our services cater to various industries and organizations across Malawi.

On-Site Interpretation

Skilled interpreters available for meetings, conferences, and public events for all types of entities.

Virtual Interpretation

Remote sign language interpretation via video calls for flexibility and convenience.

Workplace Accessibility

Interpretation services for corporate meetings, trainings, and employee support.

About Us

Tsogolo Lowala

Tsogolo lowala Deaf Foundation is committed to empowering Deaf women, youth, and students by providing a platform for technical, social, and academic interaction. Our mission is to eliminate barriers to education and economic opportunities caused by communication challenges. We advocate for a conducive business environment and equal opportunities for Deaf individuals in Malawi by promoting small businesses, facilitating access to financial literacy, and collaborating with stakeholders to enhance vocational training for the Deaf community.
